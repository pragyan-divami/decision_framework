CBAM Refinancing vs Green-Steel Capex Deferral

EU CBAM on Indian flat steel exports is expected to add roughly €140M per year to European exposure just as a €900M green-steel tranche requires sign-off. The available calls are not equally attractive: raise a sustainability-linked bond at 7.2%, defer €300M of capex by 9 months, or split the move. The role lens here is balance-sheet discipline, financing credibility, and protection of the broader green-steel transformation thesis.
