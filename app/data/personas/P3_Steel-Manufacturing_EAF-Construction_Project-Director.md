## Role

EAF Project Director, Tata Steel UK. Site-based at Port Talbot. Reports to the CEO (P1) and to Tata Steel group capital projects governance in Mumbai. Day-to-day owner of the £1.25B construction programme.

---

## Background

Dr Owen Griffiths is a composite persona representing the project director function that Tata Steel UK appointed to manage the physical delivery of the EAF. He interfaces daily with Tenova (the primary EAF contractor, signed October 2024), ABB (electrical systems and automation), and Clecim (downstream ladle metallurgy). He is also managing the demolition of blast furnace infrastructure and site preparation while the hot strip mill and downstream processing centres remain live.

Construction broke ground July 14 2025. The EAF will be installed in the existing Basic Oxygen Steelmaking (BOS) plant building. Key technical parameters: 320 tonnes of molten metal tapped every 42 minutes; two new ladle furnaces for further processing; new scrap handling facility and scrap yards; underground and overground electrical infrastructure to support the National Grid connection.
